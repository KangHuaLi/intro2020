Biography

I join the MediaTek as Computer Vision and Deep Learning Research Engineer in winter, 2019. My research interests include deep learning and generative model.

During my colleage, I join the research team of Embedded System and Application Lab (ESA-Lab) which is host by Prof. Bor-Shing Lin. The research topic I consider is the deep learning object recognition system. During my graduate studeis, I work on image-to-image translation with Prof. Yu-Tai Ching.

In the meanwhile, I had the experience to do the research and cooperated with the Prof. Wei-Chen Chiu who is the leader of Enriched Vision Applications Lab (EVA-Lab). Finally, I also investigated some research topic on music with Dr. Li Su at National Tsing Hua University.

To fullfill my career goal, I am finding any AI research cooperation oppertunity within my research interests.
